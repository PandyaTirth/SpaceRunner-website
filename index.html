Digital Floor Plans: Space Management Evolved

With SpaceRunner, you will create a Digital Floor Plan for every physical space in organization.
With your Digital Floor Plans, you will:

  • Visualize every physical space in your organization.
  • Modify your spaces through a drag-and-drop interface.
  • Draw boundaries to define buildings, floors, departments, and rooms.
  • Plot assets directly on your Digital Floor Plans themselves.
  • Access all asset details, documents, and data from your Digital Floor Plans.
  • Share and sync Digital Floor Plans in real-time across your entire organization.
  • Perform all space management from the Digital Floor Plans themselves.

How Digital Floor Plans Work

SpaceRunner's Digital Floor Plans put everything you need to perform modern space management in one place. They will give you a visual tactile and intutive drag-and-drop interface that any
non-technical user can quickly learn and deploy to perform all space management activities in one place.

Digital Floorplans are fast, easy, and simple to setup.

You can rapidly create a Digital Floor Plan for any physical space in your
organization through multiple methods:

  • You can directly pull existing floor plans from BIM/Revit.
  • You can upload your space data from PDFs or Excel.
  • You can draw them from scratch within SpaceRunner.
  • You can ask our professional services team to create them for you.

After you have created one or more Digital Floor Plans, you can populate
them with assets represented by Smart Icons.

  • You can make anything a Smart Icon— devices, outlets, equipment,
    or people.
  • You can create Smart Icons from scratch, or upload asset data from
    Excel.
  • You can attach any type of infromation, details, or documents
    directly to assets.
  • You can make Smart Icons look like their assets for easy discovery.
  • You can place Smart Icons exactly where they are located in their
    spaces.

Finally, once you have created your spaces, loaded your assets, and
connected your data sources, you can perform a complete suite of space
management activities from a single platform, directly from Digital Floor
PLans themselves.

  • You can drag-and-drop assets within your Floor Plan, or across multiple
    Plans.
  • You can access and modify your spaces directly from your Floor Plans.
  • You can visualize space utilization data as heat maps on your Floor Plans.
  • You can access SpaceRunner from any device with a WiFi signal.

SpaceRunner can automatically collect all of the data generated by
your spaces and your assets, store that data in the platform, and
provide robust reporting.

  • You can develop complete, centralized visibility into all space and
    asset data..
  • You can collect usage & behavior data from integrated sensors & IoT
    devices.
  • You can manage data at the individual space or asset level, or
    holistically.
  • You can visualize your space & asset data through custom
    dashboards.
  • You can create custom reports that run any usage data you desire in
    one click.
